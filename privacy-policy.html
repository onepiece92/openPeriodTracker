Privacy Policy

Open Period & Cycle Tracker · Last updated: March 27, 2026

All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal or health data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • We do not collect health or medical data from your device
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not require user accounts, registration, or login
  • We do not transmit any data to remote servers
  • We do not use advertising or ad networks
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not sell user data

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database:

Health & Cycle Data

  • Period start and end dates
  • Flow intensity (light, medium, heavy)
  • Mood and symptom selections
  • Medical checklist responses (pain, discharge, sleep, skin, energy, digestion, weight, libido, breast changes, hair changes)
  • Free-text notes

Personal Information (Optional)

  • Name and nickname (used only for in-app greeting)
  • Birthday (used only to calculate age for age-appropriate health insights)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Location Data

The App may request access to your device's location to provide region-appropriate diet and food recommendations.

  • Location data is processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Never stored permanently
  • You can deny permission — the App works fully without it

Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you when your predicted period date has passed. Notifications are generated entirely on your device and are fully optional.

Optional AI Features

On-Device Analysis

Health summaries and cycle insights run entirely on your device using rule-based algorithms. No network connection required.

Ollama Integration (Optional)

You can optionally connect to your own local Ollama LLM server for enhanced AI summaries. No data is sent to any external server.

AI Prompt Builder

The App generates text prompts you can manually copy to external AI services. This is entirely user-initiated.

Data Security

  • Local-only storage — Data exists only on your physical device
  • No network transmission — Core features make zero network calls
  • No cloud backup — Data is not backed up to any cloud service
  • Device encryption — Data benefits from your device's built-in encryption
  • User-controlled export — Only you can export your data

Data Retention & Deletion

  • All data stored locally for as long as you use the App
  • Delete all data anytime via "Reset All Data" in Profile
  • Uninstalling the App removes all data
  • We retain no copies on any server

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. No account creation or personal information is required.

Medical Disclaimer: This App provides health insights for informational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
